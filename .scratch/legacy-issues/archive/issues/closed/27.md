Z-fighting or something when expanding role types in a lobby

**FIXED** 2025-07-11

The general expand/close properly places and animates the role types when expanding/closing, but the following happens as well only on the first expand/collapse of the given procedure:

- expand Leaders role types
- Toggle one of the role types
- Collapse leaders role types
- Observe that for a brief instant, the individual leader roles appear occupying the same x/y spot as the "Leaders" label and whatnot.

It's impossible for me to capture a screenshot of this, unfortunately. We may need to set up some mutationobservers?

## Resolution Notes

**Root Cause**: During collapse animation, individual role cards were briefly visible at the parent position because the collapse content was still rendered while the container was transitioning from expanded to collapsed state.

**Solution**: Added `data-show` directive to the role cards container that binds to the accordion state. This causes Datastar to instantly hide the content when the accordion checkbox is unchecked, preventing the z-fighting effect.

**Implementation**: 
- File: `nix/app/internal/views/components/role_config_new.templ:168`
- Added `data-show={ fmt.Sprintf("$accordion%s", typeName) }` to the cards container
- This ensures content disappears immediately on collapse rather than waiting for CSS animation
